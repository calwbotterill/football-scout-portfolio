Match Overview
This report covers individual Birmingham City U18 player assessments from the Crystal Palace U18 fixture, focused on role profile, technical and tactical actions, physical traits, and key decision-making moments throughout the match.
Player Assessments
Terenowicz
Goalkeeper Age 18 Approx 6'2 Right Footed
Comfortable in possession and willing to play short, however long distribution lacked quality and was intercepted on multiple occasions. Communication with the back line during goal-kicks was limited, leading to uncertainty in build-up. Played some careless passes into midfield which resulted in turnovers. Dealt well with aerial deliveries, producing a strong punch from one corner and confidently collecting another. Showed good reactions to make an excellent reflex save, parrying the ball onto the post. Organisation at defensive set-plays appeared weak, with overall responsibility unclear as multiple goals were conceded from corners.
Drammeh
Right-Back Age 16 Approx 6'0 Right Footed
Athletic right-back with good speed and strength. Defensively showed good anticipation, making a number of interceptions and a strong tackle near his own corner flag. Continued this after the break with a well-timed sliding tackle and generally competed well in duels. In possession his throw-ins were poor throughout, committing multiple foul throws and frequently releasing the ball with excessive pace which led to possession lost. Going forward had limited opportunities.
Ranson
Right Centre-Back Age 16 Approx 5'10 Left Footed
Physically strong centre-back who competed well aerially, winning multiple headers and clearances. Comfortable stepping in with possession, carrying the ball into midfield and finding passes into central areas, including a clever line-breaking pass to initiate a counterattack. Showed composure under pressure, and willing to block shots with his body. Defensively at fault for the opening goal, allowing a ball to be flicked over his head easily; agility could be a weakness. Later conceded a penalty by rashly kicking an opponent after a loose pass, overall capable on the ball but concentration and decision-making inconsistent at key moments.
B Campbell
Left Centre-Back Age 16 Approx 6'2 Left Footed
Physically strong defender who competed well aerially. Produced a good 1v1 tackle preventing the striker from advancing a wide area. Enjoys receiving under pressure and capable of dribbling out of situations. However, decision-making on the ball was inconsistent, showing overconfidence when turning and losing possession in dangerous areas. Overall strong physically but concentration and risk management need improvement.
Salif Bamba
Left-Back Age 17 Approx 5'8 Both Footed
Technically comfortable and able to use both feet, showed quality with forward passes including a well-weighted ball in behind. Combined effectively at times with bounce passes and 1-2s to progress play and showed good positioning when defending 2v1 situations, making an important clearance and interception. Defensively inconsistent, occasionally too relaxed when facing crosses and failing to engage quickly enough. Lost aerial duels against taller opponents and repeatedly gave away possession with poor throw-ins. Lost awareness of his runner during a counterattack and again from a corner which resulted in a goal. Tendency to overuse bounce passes led to turnovers and left him out of position defensively.
Guernier
Left-Mid Age 17 Approx 5'7 Right Footed
Attacking wide player who looks to dribble and take on defenders. Shows awareness to switch play and offers creativity in possession. Attempted a shot from a difficult position, understandable given limited team chance creation. Struggled when pressed, finding it difficult to retain possession under pressure and offered limited defensive contribution, rarely engaging in tackles.
Martin-Moore
Centre-Mid Age 18 Approx 5'8 Right Footed
Energetic and athletic midfielder with very good speed and agility. Anticipates play well defensively and shows a natural tendency to think about defensive positioning. Carries the ball forward effectively and looks to progress play quickly, passing with intent rather than circulating possession. Works hard for the team and displays a strong attitude throughout. Technically still raw at times but shows clear potential due to physical profile and work rate.
Ralph
Centre-Mid Age 16 Approx 6'0 Left Footed
Central playmaker who regularly scans and looks to dictate play. Comfortable on the ball, producing a clever reverse pass, a well-weighted long ball in behind and a composed lay-off under pressure inside the box. Combined effectively with the left-back and showed willingness to control tempo. However, awareness inconsistent in deeper possession areas; a sloppy pass led to a penalty and a dangerous counterattack in separate scenarios. Occasionally avoided physical challenges and appeared slightly lightweight. Later moved to left-back during the second half showing versatility.
Nunes-Wickham
Right-Mid Age 17 Approx 6'0 Left Footed
Direct winger with good pace and a composed first touch. Immediately attacked space when receiving, cutting inside onto his left foot to produce a good shot. Demonstrated vision with a switch of play and showed technical quality in dribbling situations. Defensively mixed - showed strong tenacity to win the ball at times but also turned his back in defensive moments and occasionally overcommitted in tackles, allowing opponents to bypass him.
Elmansuri
Centre-Attack-Mid Age 17 Approx 5'7 Right Footed
Technically comfortable in tight areas, showing good close control and willingness to receive the ball. Delivered a couple of dangerous crosses into the box and consistently looked to get involved in possession. Defensive contribution questionable, appealing for fouls instead of reacting in transition and occasionally careless with possession and poor in aerial duels.
Degtiarev
Striker Age 16 Approx 5'9 Right Footed
Hard-working forward who pressed defenders consistently and showed a strong attitude throughout. Looked to run in behind and produced a good half-turn touch followed by a well-weighted through ball. Struggled with hold-up play, frequently losing possession when receiving with back to goal. More effective attacking space than receiving to feet. Also took set-pieces.
